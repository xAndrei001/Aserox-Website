Who We Are

About Team ASEROX

ASEROX is a competitive Counter-Strike 2 team forged in the fires of online matchmaking and fueled by a passion for tactical gameplay and teamwork. We primarily focus on the Faceit platform, striving to climb the ranks and make a name for ourselves.

Our journey started with a group of friends who shared a common goal: to play CS2 at a high level while maintaining a positive and supportive team environment. We believe in constant improvement, strategic thinking, and, most importantly, enjoying the game.
